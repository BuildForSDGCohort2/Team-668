About Us

HomeShop

We have built our company on the core principle that our customers are our priority inspired during the COVID-19 pandemic. The safety and health of our customers is what matters the most to us. Therefore it is important to ensure that our customers can enjoy shopping from just a touch of a button and have their goods delivered to their doorstep, without having the risk of being exposed to the virus. HomeShop is all about innovation and prioritizing the customers needs by developing tech that will meet their needs.

All our customers deserve only the best services, and therefore the disability community inspired HomeShop to cater only the best and fast services. Our mission is to ensure that the disabled community can enjoy shopping for their goods and have it delivered to them with ease. We are all about the customers and what this online-shopping experience will bring is comfort and accessibility.
